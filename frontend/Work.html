How This Works

1. The Invisible Layer

Computers don't "see" images like humans do. They see a grid of numbers. We take your image (the superficial layer) and add a specifically calculated "digital mist." To your eyes, the photo looks exactly the same. To an AI, the numbers are now scrambled, making it impossible to interpret the image content.
